    Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computer systems, networks, and digital information from unauthorized access, theft, damage, or other malicious attacks. With the increasing reliance on digital technology in our personal and professional lives, cybersecurity has become an essential aspect of protecting sensitive information and assets. Cybersecurity encompasses a wide range of practices and technologies, such as firewalls, antivirus software, encryption, intrusion detection and prevention systems, access control, and security policies and procedures. It also involves staying informed about the latest threats and vulnerabilities, and taking proactive measures to mitigate risks and respond to security incidents. Cybersecurity is crucial for individuals, businesses, and governments alike, as cyberattacks can result in significant financial losses, reputational damage, and even physical harm. As technology continues to evolve and new threats emerge, cybersecurity will remain an important focus area for organizations and individuals around the world..
