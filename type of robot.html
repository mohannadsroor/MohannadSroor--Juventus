    • Welding robots

    • Palletizing robots

  9. Entertainment robots:
    • Theme park robots
    • Stage show robots
    • Interactive exhibit robots
    • Party entertainers

  • Industrial robots

    Industrial robots are used in manufacturing and other industrial settings. They are typically designed to perform repetitive tasks, such as welding or assembly, with a high degree of accuracy and precision.

  • Service robots

    Service robots are designed to interact with people and perform tasks that are helpful to humans. Examples include vacuum cleaners, pool cleaners, and personal assistants.

  • Military robots

    Military robots are used for a variety of military applications, such as bomb disposal, surveillance, and transportation of equipment.
